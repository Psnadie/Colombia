How can we help?

Colombia Tourism is the official tourism agency of Colombia. We assist international visitors with trip planning, booking coordination, press credentials, and accessibility support.

Our team is based across five offices in Colombia and works in English, Spanish, French, and Portuguese.

We respond to all enquiries within 2 business days. For urgent matters, please indicate "Urgent" in your message.

How to arrive

Reaching Colombia

Colombia is served by four international airports, with direct connections from North America, Europe, and Latin America. Entry requirements vary by nationality.

Major international airports

  • BOG
    El Dorado International Airport
    Bogotá D.C. — Primary international hub
  • MDE
    José María Córdova International Airport
    Rionegro (Medellín) — Second largest hub
  • CTG
    Rafael Núñez International Airport
    Cartagena de Indias — Caribbean gateway
  • CLO
    Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport
    Palmira (Cali) — Pacific region gateway

Airline connections

North America
American Airlines (Miami, New York, Dallas) · United Airlines (Houston, Newark) · Delta (Atlanta) · Avianca (multiple US cities) · Spirit (Fort Lauderdale)
Europe
Iberia (Madrid) · Air France (Paris) · Lufthansa (Frankfurt) · KLM (Amsterdam) · Avianca (Madrid, Barcelona, London) · Wingo (seasonal)
Latin America
LATAM (Lima, Buenos Aires, Santiago, São Paulo) · Avianca (regional network) · Copa Airlines (Panama City hub) · Wingo (regional low-cost)

Entry requirements & visas

Colombia does not require a visa for citizens of most countries. The standard tourist stay is 90 days per calendar year, with the possibility of a single 90-day extension at the Migración Colombia office.

Region / Country group Entry requirement Max stay
European Union Visa-free 90 days
United States & Canada Visa-free 90 days
United Kingdom Visa-free 90 days
Latin America (Andean & Mercosur) Visa-free 90 days
Australia & New Zealand Visa-free 90 days
Japan, South Korea Visa-free 90 days
China, India, Russia Tourist visa required 30–90 days
All other nations Check with Colombian consulate Varies
Entry requirements: All visitors require a valid passport (minimum 6 months validity), a return or onward ticket, and proof of sufficient funds. Yellow fever vaccination certificate required for travellers arriving from or transiting through yellow fever endemic countries. Visa information is subject to change — verify with the nearest Colombian consulate before travel.
